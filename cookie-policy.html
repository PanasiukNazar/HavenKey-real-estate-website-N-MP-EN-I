Cookie Policy - Real Estate Agency Website

On our website, dedicated to real estate services and property listings, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. To enhance the experience of our visitors and clients, we use cookies on our website. 1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or other internet-enabled device) when you visit a website. These files contain information that allows websites to remember your activities or preferences over time. Cookies are widely used to improve website functionality, make browsing more efficient, and provide a personalized user experience.

There are different types of cookies used for various purposes. Some cookies are essential for the website to function properly, while others track user behavior to improve the site or provide targeted advertising.

  • Security: Some cookies help ensure the security of our website, including verifying user identities and protecting against fraudulent activities.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Below is an overview of the main categories of cookies used on our real estate website:

3.1. Necessary Cookies

Necessary cookies are essential for the proper operation of our website. Without these cookies, certain parts of the site may not be accessible or function correctly. These cookies do not require your consent and are enabled automatically when you visit our site.

Examples of necessary cookies we use:

  • Session Cookies: These cookies help the website remember your activities during your browsing session, such as keeping you logged in as you navigate between pages.
  • Security Cookies: We use cookies to ensure that users are authorized to access certain areas, like personal accounts or inquiry forms.

3.2. Performance Cookies

Performance cookies help us analyze how visitors use our website. They collect anonymous data to understand which areas of the site work well and which need improvement.

Examples of performance cookies we use:

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device. You can disable or block cookies anytime via your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Hereâ€™s how to manage cookies on common browsers:
