Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Privacy Policy. Protecting your privacy is our priority.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how we collect, protect, and use personal information ("Personal Data") you ("User," "You," or "Your") provide when visiting our real estate agency website or using our services related to property buying, selling, or renting. It also outlines your options regarding your data and how you can access or update it. This Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services not controlled by us.

2. Collection of Personal Data

We collect and store any personal information you willingly provide when you fill out inquiry forms, register for property alerts, or contact us for real estate services. You may choose not to provide certain information, but this may limit your access to some services or property listings.

3. Collection of Non-Personal Data

When you visit our website, our servers automatically record information your browser sends. This may include your device's IP address, browser type and version, operating system, language preferences, the webpage you visited before our site, pages viewed on our site, time spent on those pages, search queries for properties, access times and dates, and other statistics.

4. Use of Collected Information

The information we gather is used to improve customer service, respond to your inquiries, manage property listings, and operate our website efficiently. Non-personal data helps us detect misuse and create aggregated statistics on website usage without identifying individual users.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies to gather data for analytical purposes, improve user experience, and manage our real estate services online. You can choose to accept or decline cookies. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies if you prefer.

6. Google Ads and Data Collection

Our website utilizes Google Ads to display relevant property advertisements. Google Ads uses cookies to tailor ads based on your previous visits to our website or others. Google may use this data to provide you with personalized ads and share it with other Google services. You can opt out of Google Ads personalization by visiting the Google Ads Settings page. To disable personalized Google ads completely, visit the opt-out page at https://optout.networkadvertising.org/.

7. Security of Your Information

We store your information on secure servers within controlled environments designed to protect against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of your personal data related to real estate transactions and inquiries.

8. Links to External Websites

Our website may contain links to external real estate platforms or partners not owned or managed by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal data.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our real estate services, legal requirements, or operational practices.
